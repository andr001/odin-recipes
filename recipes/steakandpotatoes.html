Steak and Potatoes

Description

A classic dinner option that is both satisfying and delicious

Ingredients

  • Steak: Cant go wrong with a New York Strip, or Filet Mignon cut
  • Potatoes: I prefer a mix
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Rosemary or thyme
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Chop potatoes to desired size
  3. If you have a sous vide, get it out and preheat the water to 285 degrees
  4. Season the steaks generously with salt and pepper on all sides
  5. If using a sousvide, place the steaks into vaccum sealed bags with a slice of butter and your herbs
  6. Place the sealed bags into the circulating water and allow to cook for at least an hour
  7. Once your steaks have enjoyed their bath, set them aside while you preheat a cast iron skillet with a small ammount of olive oil in it over medium-low heat
  8. Toss the potatoes with olive oil and salt and pepper, use some of the rosemary or thyme as well but save enough for basting your stakes later
  9. Place the potatoes on a baking sheet and insert into the oven for 20 minutes
  10. When your potatoes are 10 minutes from complete, turn the heat up for the skillet and begin searing your steaks
  11. Give each side of each steak enough time to develop a crust
  12. Add liquid from the sousvide bags and an additional slice of butter or bit of olive oil, add herbs
  13. Baste steaks until they have achieved your desired crust level
  14. Allow steaks to rest while you pull the potatoes out and plate them, slice and serve steaks
  15. Enjoy!
