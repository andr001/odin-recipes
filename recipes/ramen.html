Ramen

Description

Nothing warms the soul or fills the stomach quite like a bowl of ramen, here is my recipe for a delicious and easy bowl. I take a lot of shortcuts with this recipe because I am usually pressed for time when I make it. Please take the following as a general guideline.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Broth Mix
  • Scallions
  • Corn
  • Cha Shu pork
  • Seaweed
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Set out two pots each filled with water
  2. Set one pot to medium high heat and allow to come to a boil
  3. Add your eggs and cook for 6 minutes (exact time will be dependent on many factors including altitude, temp, and egg quality.. 6 minutes works for me
  4. While the eggs cook, prepare an ice bath by putting ice and cold water in a medium sized bowl
  5. After your eggs are cooked to your liking, pull them out and place them in the ice to halt the cooking. Shell them when they are cold enough to handle
  6. In the second bowl, your selected broth base will be added and brought up to a low boil
  7. Add in any ingredients to the broth that you would like to simmer, I add the scallions and corn
  8. Rinse your egg pot and fill it with water, bring it to a quick boil over high heat
  9. Add your noodles to the boiling water and cook them till just a hair over al dente
  10. Strain your noodles and add them to the broth with all remaining ingredients
  11. Season to your liking and serve while hot
  12. Enjoy!
